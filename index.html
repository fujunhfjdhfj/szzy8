简介/introduction


当资本写在脸上成为现实，人类社会将如何运作？


22世纪，人们习惯了线上虚拟生活，“数字造颜”早已成为生活常态，在此看脸的时代，华丽的数字颜即是高贵身份的象征，因而不同价位的造颜技术自然地将人类分化为“上中下”三个身份阶层，而作为当下最火热的偶像，“X”在人们心中有着至高无上的地位，一项神秘的技术让他的真容无法显露，不同的人看到他有着不同的面容，就像100个人心中有100个哈姆雷特那样，“X”是千人千面的代名词。今日，“X”的表演又将在此进行，而千人千面的背后，究竟是什么？
 
——————————————————————————————————————————————————————————

In the 22nd century, people were used to online virtual life, "digital face making" has long become the norm of life. In this era of looking at faces, gorgeous digital face is a symbol of noble identity. Therefore, face making technology at different prices naturally divides human beings into three identity classes. As the hottest idol at present, "X" has a supreme position in people's hearts, A mysterious technology makes it impossible to reveal his true face. Different people see that he has different faces, just like 100 Hamlets in 100 people's hearts, "X" is synonymous with thousands of people and thousands of faces. Today, the performance of "X" will be performed here. What is behind thousands of people and thousands of faces?
